# Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

This project analyzes retail sales performance using interactive dashboards built in **Power BI and Microsoft Excel**.  
The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights for better business decision-making.

---

## Project Overview

The dashboards provide a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profitability trends, customer contribution, and underperforming product categories.

Key business questions addressed:

- What are the overall sales, profit, and order trends?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which sub-categories are underperforming?
- Which customers contribute the most revenue?
- How are sales trends evolving over time?

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Business Analysis

---

## Dataset

The dataset used is the **Superstore Sales dataset**, which contains information about:

- Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Sales
- Profit
- Regions
- Categories

---

# Excel Dashboard

The Excel dashboard provides a detailed overview of sales performance with interactive filters.

### Features

- KPI Cards for **Total Sales, Total Profit, and Total Orders**
- Sales trend analysis over time
- Category-wise sales comparison
- Profit analysis by region
- Top 10 customers by sales
- Interactive filters for region, category, and year

### Key Insights

- Technology category generates the highest revenue
- West region shows the strongest profitability
- Sales trend shows growth after 2016

---

# Power BI Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard focuses on advanced business insights and interactive visualization.

### Features

- KPI indicators for **Total Sales, Profit, Orders, and Profit Margin**
- Sales trend analysis
- Sales vs Profit comparison by sub-category
- Pareto analysis for customer revenue concentration
- Identification of underperforming sub-categories

### Key Insights

- Technology category drives the highest revenue
- Some sub-categories such as **Tables** generate negative profit
- A small percentage of customers contribute to a large portion of revenue (Pareto principle)

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-performing categories like **Technology**
- Investigate pricing and discount strategies for loss-making sub-categories
- Improve targeted marketing toward high-value customers
- Optimize inventory planning based on sales trends
